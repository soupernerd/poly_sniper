# Commercial Licensing

PolySnipe community source is non-commercial by default under `LICENSE`.

If you want to use PolySnipe commercially (hosted services, paid signals, client work, paid products, internal business deployment), you need a separate paid commercial license.

## Request a Commercial License

Contact the project owner to request terms and pricing.

Suggested info to include:
- your company / project name
- intended use case
- estimated users / deployment model
- whether you need custom support

Until a commercial agreement is signed, commercial use is not permitted.
